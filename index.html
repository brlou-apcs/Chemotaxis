Realistic Simulation of Bacteria in Pool Water*

They grow! They Live! They Exponentially Multiply! They Die!



Dev: Brandon Lou for Lowell HS' AP Computer Science Class.
Uses an ArrayList to store all instances of Bacteria.
*Chlorine doesn't kill all... beware of ebola or zika or swine flu or bubonic plague or smallpox... you know the point. The world is infested with bacteria.
